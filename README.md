## 🚀 Take Your Roblox Experience to the Next Level
Is the default Roblox launcher too limiting? **Bloxstrap** completely redefines how you play. It is a powerful, lightweight, and completely free open-source utility built to give you total control over your gaming environment. Optimize performance, customize visuals, and eliminate lag in just one click.

---

## 🔥 Key Features & Benefits

* **⚡ Ultimate FPS Boost & Optimization:** Edit `FastFlags` directly through a user-friendly interface. Set custom frame rate limits (built-in FPS Unlocker), disable heavy post-processing for low-end PCs, and reduce network latency.
* **🎮 Advanced Discord Rich Presence:** Show your friends exactly what game/place you are playing, display elapsed time, and allow them to join your server instantly without exposing your account cookies.
* **🎨 Full Customization & Modding:** Bring back the classic old death sound (Oof), apply custom mouse cursors, modify fonts, install textures, and even restore legacy launcher themes. Your mods stay intact even after Roblox updates.
* **🛠️ Server & Integration Management:** Easily track which region/server you are connected to, get notified of server locations, and manage third-party integrations like a pro.

## 🛑 Security & Ban Safety
> **Important Note:** Bloxstrap is **not** a cheat, exploit, or injector. It does not modify game memory or violate Roblox's Terms of Service (ToS). It acts purely as an external wrapper and bootstrapper, making it 100% safe for your account.
